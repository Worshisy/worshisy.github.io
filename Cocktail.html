Old-Fashioned

Classic recipe: 2oz bourbon, 1 teaspoon syrup, 2 dash bitters.

Daiquiri

Classic recipe: 2oz white rum, 1oz lime juice, 0.75 oz simple syrup. Garnish: line wedge.

Extended Family:

Mojito:

  • Mojito: 10 mint leaves, 0.75 oz simple syrup, 1 white sugar cube, 2 oz white rum, 1oz lime juice.Garnish: 1 mint bouquet.
  • Jack Froest: replace rum in mojito by tequila+brandy+dry vermouth.

Mai Tai:

  • Mai Tai: 1oz gold rum, 1oz white rum, 1 oz lime juice, 0.5oz orgeat (杏仁) syrup, 0.25oz simple syrup, 1 dash bitters. Garnish: 1 mint bouquet.
  • Zombie Punch: 2.5oz gold/blend rum, 0.5oz 151 rum, 0.5oz Donn’s Mix Syrup (cinnamon+syrup+grape), 1oz of lime/fruit juice, 2 dash pernod absinthe (苦艾酒). Garnish: special cup + fruits/bouquet.

Tom Collins:

Different variations: 1. with ginger bear/syrup; 2. with egg white; 3. with Champagne/Sparkling.

  • Tom Collins: 2oz cold seltzer, 2oz gin, 1oz lemon juice, 0.75 oz syrup. Garnish: orange half-wheel + cherry.
  • Moscow Mule1: 2oz cold seltzer (ginger bear if w/o ginger syrup), 2oz vodka, 0.5 oz lime juice, 0.75 oz ginger syrup. Garnish: orange half-wheel + candied ginger.
  • Dark and Stormy1: 2oz cold seltzer, 2oz black rum, 0.75 oz lime juice, 1 oz ginger syrup. Garnish: orange half-wheel + candied ginger.
  • Presbyterian1: 2oz cold seltzer, 2oz rye, 1 oz lime & lemon juice, 0.75 oz ginger syrup. Garnish: line wedge.
  • Silver Fizz2: 2oz gin, 0.75oz simple syrup, 0.75oz lime juice, 1 egg white, 2oz cold seltzer. Garnish: none.
  • Ramos Gin Fizz2: 2oz gin, 1oz simple syrup, 1oz lime & lemon juice, 1oz heavy cream, 1 egg white, 3 drops orange flower water, 2oz cold seltzer. Garnish: none.
  • French 753: 1oz gin, 0.5oz lemon juice, 0.5oz simple syrup, 4 oz cold dry sparkling wine. Garnish: 1 lemon twist.

Daiquiri Variations:

  • Whiskey Sour: 2oz bourbon, 0.75oz lemon juice, 0.75oz simple syrup. Garnish: 1 lemon wedge.
  • Bee's Knees: 2oz (London dry) gin, 0.75oz lemon juice, 0.75oz honey syrup.
  • Pink Lady: 1.5oz (Plymouth) gin, 0.5 oz apple brandy, 0.5oz lemon juice, 0.5oz grenadine syrup, 0.5 oz simple syrup, 1 egg white. Garnish: 1 brandied cherry on a skewer.
  • Jack Rose: 2oz apple brandy, 0.75 oz lime juice, 0.75oz grenadine syrup. Garnish: 1 apple slice.
  • Brown Derby: 2oz bourbon, 1oz grapefruit juice, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 0.5oz honey syrup.
  • Southside: 5 mint leaves (muddled), 2oz (Plymouth) gin, 0.75oz lime juice, 0.75oz simple syrup, 1 dash of Angostura bitters. Garnish: 1 mint leaf.

Other Daiquiri:

  • Pisco Sour: 2oz pisco, 0.5oz lemon & lime juice, 0.75 simple syrup, 1 egg white. Garnish: 3 drops Angostura bitters.
  • Hemingway Daiquiri: 1.5oz white rum, 0.5oz Maraschino liqueur (樱桃酒), 1oz grapefruit juice, 1oz lime juice, 1 teaspoon simple syrup. Garnish: 1 lime wedge.
  • Amaretto Sour: 2oz Amaretto (苦杏仁酒), 1oz lemon juice, 0.25 simple syrup, 1 dash of Angostura bitters. Garnish: 1 orange half wheel, 1 brandied cherry on a skewer.
  • Fresh Gimlet: 2oz (Plymouth) gin, 1oz lime juice, 0.75 oz simple syrup. Garnish: 1 lime wedge.

Sidecar

Classic recipe: 1.5oz cognac, 1oz cointreau, 0.75oz lemon juice. Garnish: 1 orange twist.
