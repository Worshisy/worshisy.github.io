About Me:

Greetings, I am Yi Shen, a first-year PhD student at the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE), University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. My research interests are Mixed-signal & Communication Integrated Circuits

Since February 2023, I have been actively engaged in research at the Michigan Integrated Circuits Laboratory (MICL), working under the esteemed supervision of Prof. David Blaauw. Additionally, I actively collaborate with Prof. Hun-Seok Kim. I am passionate about advancing knowledge in the Integrated Circuits & VLSI field and look forward to making meaningful contributions throughout my academic journey.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a keen interest in photography. You can explore my work by clicking here. Beyond photography, I find joy in traveling, crafting delightful coffee and cocktails, experimenting with cooking, and watching different kinds of movies. Badminton and soccer rank as my favorite sports activities.
